About Molly Meow Cat

Molly Meow Cat started Kitty-To-Go when she felt tired of being neglected at home by her irresponsible owner who left her home alone all the time and could only spend time with her a few hours a week. Her neighborhood cat friends rallied together and pooled their resources to launch a business where they could capitalize on their owners' negligence. Kitty-To-Go started five years ago with only a handful of curious customers, but it has grown and is now over 5,000 customers strong.
